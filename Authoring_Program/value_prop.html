Turn Courses into Cash—Become a 360 Author


  • You’re an expert in your field with years of hands-on experience. You’re great at explaining things to people and the one they come to for help and guidance. You’re known and respected within the industry—but you know you can do more. It’s time to expand your reach, build courses that can be accessed around the world, and launched through multiple vendor channels. A steady stream of royalty checks can be a beautiful thing. And helping people learn? Priceless.


    FREE Software — Get Started

1. BUILD

First, we’ll need to get some of your expertise in a course that can be sold.

  • Classroom and Webinars

    Event-based courses require a bit more scheduling for both you and your students—but some content demands live interaction. Our tools can help you get your classroom and webinar based training to market. Just fill out information about the course and publish. Submit a course today!

  • Online Courses

    Online courses enable people to learn anytime, anywhere—super convenient for them, and for you as well! Online courses provide you the opportunity to perfect your content and reach huge audiences. If you’re already a pro at making courses and have a tool you love that exports to SCORM 1.2, you are welcome to use that—we can import that content. But if you’re just getting started, let us help you transform know-how into “train now!” with our course builder. The core functionality is FREE—everything you have to have to develop an online course—plus options you can add on when you need them!

2. DISTRIBUTE

Next, for online courses, you’ll make some offers.

  • To tell 360 and our reseller network about your course, make an offer on the course builder marketplace tab. You specify royalty and lowest price. Acceptance of the offer allows recipients to sell your course to customers—but the course itself, its intellectual property, and responsibility for maintaining that course, all remain with you. Royalties are calculated monthly. Watch for the check in the mail with a report on sales!

3. IMPROVE

Now we come full circle. Successful elearning depends on getting feedback about learner experience so that those experiences can be optimized. Our system can help.

  • Course evaluations and course ratings tell you how learners feel about a specific course. Associated reports provide quantitative data about how long it’s taking students to finish the course, and score—how well the course content is supporting performance on assessments. Drilling down, the assessment item report gives you a way to analyze performance question by question. Perfect your course, improve customer satisfaction, and increase sales.
