About Scorecards

MAKE YOUR COURSES THE VERY BEST THEY CAN BE!

For each lesson, once you earn 100 points building out that lesson—or choose to apply some of your saved points towards that lesson—you can request ﬁve peer reviews on it.
Take that feedback, perfect the course, then release it to your learners and watch the results. Win points every time an end-user gives you a neutral to positive rating—and lose points if the ratings are negative. No ratings are tallied until there are at least twenty votes on the course.

Prestige and earnings can rocket up as you hone your skills in the marketplace!
